Masked Heroes/Thank You

<%= publish_time_tag %>

a poem


masked heroes

     scrubbed in on the frontlines

          of pandemic panic

wranglers of the infirm

     and ignorant

stepping into risk knowingly

     because of a decision

          made long ago

long before

     the exponential curve

     the fake news virus

     the spread

     inflection points passed

     sporadic social distancing

striding headfirst into the theater

so that we may

     perhaps

          remain safe

          recover

          regain normalcy

thanklessly toiling

     for us

 

Thank you.
