Progress vs. Excuses

<%= publish_time_tag %>

[Humble the Poet](https://www.humblethepoet.com/) was on the [Rich Roll podcast](https://www.richroll.com/podcast/humble-the-poet-461/) dropping knowledge bombs.

Including:

If you're not making progress, you're making excuses.

I'm practically en expert on procrastinating and making grand plans with very little in the way of action so this hit home. It was also a great kick in the ass and reminder to do soemthing, anything.

You don't have to finish in one go. One day. All at once.

Even 1% is progress. And progress compounds, so 1% daily over time is a big deal.

Will you make progress or excuses today?
